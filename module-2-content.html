Ends with a soft CTA to join our email list for exclusive previews Tone: Informative yet conversational, like talking to a knowledgeable friend Keywords to include naturally: sustainable fashion, eco-friendly clothing, ethical fashion brands

The Psychology of AI Prompting

üß† Key Principle: Context is Everything

AI performs best when you provide:

  • Role: Who the AI should act as
  • Context: Background information
  • Objective: Clear goal
  • Constraints: Specific requirements
  • Format: How you want the output
